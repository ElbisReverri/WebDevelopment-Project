Killua Zoldyck

Killua Zoldyck is the second youngest member of the Zoldyck assassin family.
He and his family take on the worlds toughest jobs assassinating the highest
profile targets. Killua decided that killing was no longer for him and decided
to become a hunter where he met his best friend Gon Freecs. Together with Gon
the two went on marvelous journies that would lead to the evolution of their abilities.
Killua is known as Godspeed for his combination of eletrical nen that allow him to move
as fast as the lightning aura he produces, coupled with his own assassin abilities.
